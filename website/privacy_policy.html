Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Brevity, we believe in transparency and user privacy. As an open-source project, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being clear about how we collect, use, and share your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and chosen username. Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use Brevity, including articles read, preferences set, and app interactions to improve our service.

Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system, and app version for technical support and optimization.

How We Use Your Information

• Personalization: To provide personalized news summaries and content recommendations

• Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve Brevity's features and performance

• Communication: To send important updates about the service and respond to your inquiries

• Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

• With your explicit consent

• To comply with legal obligations or protect our legal rights

• In connection with a business transaction (merger, acquisition, etc.)

• With service providers who assist us in operating Brevity under strict confidentiality agreements

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

• Access: Request a copy of your personal data

• Correction: Update or correct your information

• Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data

• Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

• Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications

Open Source and Transparency

As an open-source project, Brevity's code is publicly available on GitHub. You can review our data handling practices, contribute to improvements, and help ensure transparency in how we manage your information.

Children's Privacy

Brevity is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Brevity after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through our contact page or reach out to our development team on GitHub.

This policy applies to the Brevity app and website. For questions about our open-source project, visit our GitHub repository.
